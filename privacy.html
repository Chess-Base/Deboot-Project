Privacy Policy for DEBoot

Last Updated: March 28, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to DEBoot, an AI-powered assistant created by xAI. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use DEBoot.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Chat Data: Messages you send to DEBoot and responses generated are stored locally in your browser to maintain chat history.
  • Uploaded Files: Text files, PDFs, or images you upload for analysis are processed temporarily and not retained after processing unless explicitly saved in chat history.
  • Preferences: Theme settings and personality choices are stored in your browser’s local storage.
  • Usage Data: Anonymized data on how you interact with DEBoot may be collected to improve our services.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Provide personalized responses based on your inputs and chat history.
  • Enhance DEBoot’s functionality and user experience.
  • Temporarily process uploaded files for analysis or summarization.

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your chat history and preferences are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. Uploaded files are processed in-memory and deleted after analysis unless stored in chat history by your action. We employ industry-standard security measures to protect data during processing, but as an AI tool, DEBoot relies on your device’s security as well.

5. Third-Party Services

DEBoot may integrate with third-party services (e.g., Puter for file storage, DuckDuckGo for web searches). These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them:

  • Puter Privacy Policy
  • DuckDuckGo Privacy Policy

6. Your Choices

You can:

  • Clear your chat history at any time using the “Clear Chat” option.
  • Opt out of text-to-speech features or adjust settings via the sidebar.
  • Delete local data by clearing your browser’s storage.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected here with an updated “Last Updated” date. Continued use of DEBoot after changes implies acceptance of the revised policy.
